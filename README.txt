[h2]Gameplay Adjustments[/h2]
[list]
[*] Max insurance companies: [b]36[/b]
[*] Max floors: [b]12[/b]
[*] Collapse and “leave hospital” timers extended (more time for multi-department diagnostics).
[*] Staff training learns significantly faster.
[*] Floor dirt rate set to [b]0[/b].
[list]
[*] With all departments, a 200+ daily patient hospital needs 800+ employees—this reduces performance pressure.
[*] You still need janitors (patient bleeding not removed).
[/list]
[/list]

[hr]
[h2]Compatibility Adjustments[/h2]
[list]
[*] ENT and Sexual Health labs removed; their tests run in the general medical lab (more realistic, lower cost).
[*] Psychology keeps only mental-health illnesses to avoid overlap with base-game Neurology.
[*] ENT sleep lab fixed—now buildable.
[*] Diagnostics surgeries fixed—can borrow surgeons from General Surgery and Neurology.
[*] Reassigned diseases to the most appropriate departments.
[*] Merged functionally identical exams; removed unused symptoms/exams/treatments to reduce doctor spam.
[*] Tuned patient-generation weights to better reflect reality.
[/list]
